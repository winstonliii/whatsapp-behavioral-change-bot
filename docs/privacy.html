Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 12, 2026

Medi ("the bot", "we", "us") is a WhatsApp chatbot that helps users maintain medication and habit routines. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. What we collect

  • Your WhatsApp phone number (used to identify your session)
  • Your stated preferences: name, timezone, wake/sleep times, reminder times, and communication tone
  • Medication adherence check-ins you send (e.g. "Y" or "N")

2. What we do not collect

  • We do not store the raw text of your messages beyond what is needed to respond
  • We do not collect medication names, diagnoses, or any clinical information
  • We do not collect payment information

3. How we use your data

Your data is used solely to deliver reminders, personalise the tone of messages, and record whether you have taken your medication. It is never sold or shared with third parties.

4. Data storage

Preferences and check-in logs are stored in a secure database accessible only to the application. Reminder schedules are stored in a Postgres database hosted on Neon.

5. Data retention and deletion

You may request deletion of all your data at any time by sending "delete my data" to the bot or by contacting us at the address below. We will remove your record within 7 days.

6. Children

Medi is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. Continued use of the bot after changes constitutes acceptance.

8. Contact

For questions or data deletion requests, open an issue at github.com/winstonliii/whatsapp-behavioral-change-bot.
